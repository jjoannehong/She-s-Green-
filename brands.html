Below are women-led period product brands that
focus on creating sustainable and environmentally-safe products!

August

August creates sustainable, ethical, and impactful pads and tampons. Their pads and tampons are made from 100% organic cotton, and are fully biodegradable!

DivaCup

Divacup focuses on creating eco-friendly, cost-effective, and chemical-free menstrual cups. They are most well-known for their “DivaCup”, a menstrual cup that can provide 12-hours of protection.

Flex

Flex features their unique FlexDisc, a menstrual disc, and FlexCup, a menstrual cup. Their flexCup is ZERO waste and their flexDisc reduces waste by 60% as compared with other period products.

GladRags

GladRags is a small business that creates reusable cloth pads. To ship these products, they use recycled, recyclable, and/or biodegradable packaging. They are also cost-effective, saving you approximately $1,500 in your lifetime!

Natracare

Natracare period products are plastic-free, chlorine-free, dye-free, and perfume-free. Their products reduce the carbon footprint generated in a year by period products by 35%.

Ruby Cup

RubyCups is a Berlin-based company that sells menstrual cups. Each time you make a purchase, they donate a Ruby Cup to a woman without access to period products. So far, they’ve donated more than 100,000 cups around the world!

Tree Hugger Cloth Pads

This small-business replaces approximately 200 disposable pads per cloth pad. Also, they plant a tree for every pantyliner and pad sold, planting thousands of trees each year!

Thinx

Thinx creates period underwear products that come in a variety of styles and sizes that can hold up to 2 tampons worth of fluid. You can wash them and re-wear them throughout your cycles, making them more sustainable than your regular disposable period products.

The Honey Pot

The Honey Pot is dedicated to creating naturally-derived menstrual products. They are cruelty-free, recyclable, and gynecologist-approved.
