Reusable Cloth Pads

In general, reusable cloth pads come with one insert and holder to be used as a regular pad. These pads are washable after every use. When deciding whether to use a pantyliner or regular inged pad, you should consider how heavy your flow is.

How to use Reusable Cloth Pads

To use a cloth pad:

  1. Place the inserts inside the holder.
  2. Snap the pad around your underwear. The smooth side of the pad goes against your skin, and the wings wrap around the crotch of the underwear to snap together.
  3. Change the complete pad (holder and inserts) every 2 to 6 hours or as needed.
